Frequently Asked Questions

How does Sinegularity work?

Sinegularity provides a platform for ideas to be shared and developed. It allows students to create a profile, search for team members, and message potential co-founders.

Who can use Sinegularity?

Sinegularity is open to all students who have start-up ideas or want to join a team.

Is Sinegularity free to use?

Yes, Sinegularity is completely free to use for all students.

Can I use Sinegularity to find a job or internship?

Yes! While the main focus of Sinegularity is to connect students with co-founders for their start-up ideas, it can also be a valuable tool for finding projects and building up your skills. Collaborating on projects with others is a great way to gain experience and build up your resume, and can often lead to future job opportunities. So whether you're looking for a co-founder or just looking to expand your skills, Sinegularity is the perfect platform for you!

What if I don't have a start-up idea, but I want to join a team?

Sinegularity is designed to help connect individuals with complementary skills and interests to develop a start-up idea. However, you can still create a profile and search for start-up ideas that interest you, or message other users to see if they are looking for team members with your skills.
